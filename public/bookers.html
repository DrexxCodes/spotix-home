Abridged Terms

How much do we charge for events?

Spotix charges 5% on every ticket sale you make in our platform

Do Spotix allows for free events?

Yes, even if your event is a free event, you can post it up on Spotix!

How much is the charge for free events?

At Spotix, we operate on a system whereby we can only gain when you gain, for a free event you don't gain anything so it's not fair to charge you. 

How many events can I post?

As many as you have!

How do I get paid from my sales?

Our dashboard allows you to monitor sales as they happen and you may receive your money AFTER the event has taken place.
