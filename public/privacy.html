PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: May 05, 2025

This Privacy Notice for for Spotix PLC (doing business as Spotix) ('we', 'us', or 'our' ), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ( 'process') your personal information when you use our services ( 'Services'), including when you:

  • Visit our website here, or any website of ours that links to this Privacy Notice.
  • Download and use our mobile application ( Spotix for users, Spotix for bookers, Spotix for admins) , or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice.
  • Engage with us in other related ways, including any sales, marketing, or events.
  • Use Spotix. Spotix is an innovative online platform designed to streamline the event planning and ticketing process for both event organizers and attendees. Our mission is to provide a seamless, user-friendly, and secure environment for discovering, planning, and attending events. Key features include:
    1. Event Creation and Management: Event planners can easily create and manage their events, including setting ticket prices, availability, and event details.
    2. Ticket Purchasing and Sales: Users can browse and purchase tickets for various events, with secure payment processing and instant ticket delivery.
    3. Event Discovery: Users can discover new events based on their interests, location, and preferences.
    4. Personalized Event Recommendations: Our algorithm provides personalized event recommendations to users based on their event history and preferences.
    5. Real-time Event Updates: Event planners can send real-time updates to attendees, ensuring everyone stays informed about event changes or cancellations.
Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. We are responsible for making decisions about how your personal information is processed. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@spotix.com.ng.

    12. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

    Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a Do-Not-Track ( 'DNT' ) feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognising and implementing DNT signals has been finalised . As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online. If a standard for online tracking is adopted that we must follow in the future, we will inform you about that practice in a revised version of this Privacy Notice.
    California law requires us to let you know how we respond to web browser DNT signals. Because there currently is not an industry or legal standard for recognising or honouring DNT signals, we do not respond to them at this time.
